How You Can Help

Volunteer Opportunities

We're always looking for passionate individuals to join our team. Here are some ways you can get involved:

  • Technology Tutors: Help teach digital literacy skills to community members
  • Device Refurbishment: Assist in cleaning and preparing donated devices
  • Program Coordinators: Help organize and run our various initiatives
  • Outreach Volunteers: Help spread awareness about our programs

Donate Devices

We accept donations of:

  • Laptops (preferably less than 5 years old)
  • Tablets
  • Smartphones
  • Computer accessories (keyboards, mice, chargers)

All devices should be in working condition or repairable.

Financial Support

As a fiscally sponsored project of Hack Club Bank, all donations to Tech Refuge are tax-deductible. Your financial support helps us:

  • Purchase necessary equipment and software
  • Fund our educational programs
  • Support our device refurbishment efforts
  • Expand our reach to more communities

Get Started

Ready to make a difference? Contact us through our contact form to learn more about current volunteer opportunities or to arrange a device donation.
