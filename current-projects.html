Current Projects

Teen Grantmaking Council Initiative

We're excited to announce that we've received a $4,500 grant from the Teen Grantmaking Council for a new program in partnership with NAI. This program will focus on providing technology education and resources to underserved communities.

NC A&T Partnership Program

We're currently developing a new program in collaboration with NC A&T University. This initiative aims to provide technology education and resources to students and community members, creating a bridge between higher education and community needs.

Refugee Technology Education

Our ongoing program focuses on teaching refugees how to use laptops and essential technology tools. This initiative helps newcomers integrate into the digital world, providing them with the skills they need for education, employment, and daily life.

Device Refurbishment Program

Our ongoing initiative to collect, refurbish, and distribute used devices to those in need. We're currently accepting donations of laptops, tablets, and smartphones.
